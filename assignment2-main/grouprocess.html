Group process and communication:

Vision Smiths typically uses the web-based application known as MS Teams for communicating. Vision Smiths primary mode of communication and way of conducting team meetings is done via video and audio. Team meetings take place on average twice weekly and as assignment due dates get closer, they may become more frequent. MS Teams is also sometimes used to share assignment-based word documents and files among team members and almost all general assignment-based text communication is done through MS Teams.

If team members cannot be contacted the team will try and use multiple avenues of communication such as Discord or may wait a few days giving the team member a chance to get in contact. If the vision Smiths team still cannot get in contact with a team member a course coordinator will have to be alerted and can hopefully get the missing team member back in contact with the rest of the team.
