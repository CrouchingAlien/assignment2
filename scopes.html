Scope and Limits

The scope of what can be developed within the time available will have to be kept rather short as the development of an AR game is complex, tedious and time consuming especially with no prior experience in the field. Designing the look and layout of the game is something that can easily be done and that can help the team have a clear vision of the project. Development of the project will come second, and this is where the scope of things becomes verry small as team members will have to develop their skill by creating the most basic aspects of the game.

The scope of design will consist of putting a simple Vr program onto a phone and getting it working and designing a menu screen for the game in unity.
