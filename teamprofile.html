How well did we work together in A2?

The group overall worked well together in assignment 2 and everyone contributed to the assignment and completed their delegated tasks. There was no conflict between members at any time which made working together and getting tasks done easy with no timewasting or disagreeing. Organization was lacking at the beginning as we were a week late starting our first meeting, but the group managed to achieve their goals and handed in a quality result. Because no one in the group knew anything about anyone when they first had come together it was clear that a solid structure for the team would be lacking.

Will we make any changes for A3?

Changes to the group in assignment 3 will be minimal as our group seemed to all get along and everyone contributed to the work. One big change that will develop through assignment 3 will be an improvement in structure with a more defined set of roles with some members taking on leadership and organizational rolls. When working as a team having a better structure really helps with better workflow and quality.
